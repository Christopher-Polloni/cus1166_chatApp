Who Are We?

TotalCollab is not your ordinary messaging application.
Common uses of TotalCollab include:
  • School Projects
  • Athletic Teams
  • Communicating With Friends
  • Business Teams
    • The list can go on and on because we are so diverse!

    What Do We Offer?

    Workspaces:
  • A place where groups get to collaborate!
  • Anybody can create one!
  • Invite Code required to join an existing workspace!

    • Subgroups:
  • A chat room within a workspace!
  • Workspace Owner and Moderators determine who has access to each subgroup!

    • Whiteboard:
  • Having trouble explaining your ideas with words? Look no further! Use our whiteboard feature to sketch out what you're trying to say.

    • Taskboard:
  • Assign tasks within each workspace to a specific member!
  • Workspace owner can send personalized email reminders!

    • User Profiles:
  • Tell everyone a little about yourself!
  • Easily search and invite other users to your workspace!
